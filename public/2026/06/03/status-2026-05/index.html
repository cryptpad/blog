We are catching up with monthly status posts after missing last month.

🌷 Spring Release

This month we launched the CryptPad Spring 2026 release (2026.5.0) with some improvements to the Diagram app among other fixes.

The diagram now defaults to a simpler user-interface theme called "sketch". This makes Diagram shine as an infinite canvas whiteboarding app by highlighting only a few keys features such as shapes, arrows, and freehand drawing. For the uses that require full bells and whistles, such as the many shape libraries available, we include a menu to switch between 3 themes.

We added additional fixes for office documents and user-registry/SSO in a 🌷🩹 Spring fix release (2026.5.1).

🎤 FossNorth

Ludovic presented CryptPad at the foss-north conference on April 27th: End to End encrypted collaboration can be easy to use. The recording is now available on Peertube and other platforms if you would like to catch up with it.

🎤 Journées du Logiciel Libre

Fabrice presented last news from CryptPad on May 30th at “Journées du Logiciel Libre” (FOSS days) in Lyon. The recording will be available soon, however the slide deck is there if you want to catch up from them (in French).

We are always happy to meet our community at such events and appreciate your ongoing support!
