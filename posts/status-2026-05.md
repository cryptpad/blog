We are catching up with monthly status posts after missing last month.

## 🌷 Spring Release

This month we launched the [CryptPad Spring 2026 release (2026.5.0)](https://github.com/cryptpad/cryptpad/releases/tag/2026.5.0) with some improvements to the Diagram app among other fixes.

![screenshot of the diagram app with a drawing: welcome to the Sketch theme! surrounded by connected shapes: Easy, Powerful, can be changed, New theme switcher! pointing to the new button in the toolbar](/images/2026-spring-release-diagram-preview.png)

The diagram now defaults to a simpler user-interface theme called "sketch". This makes Diagram shine as an infinite canvas whiteboarding app by highlighting only a few keys features such as shapes, arrows, and freehand drawing. For the uses that require full bells and whistles, such as the many shape libraries available, we include a menu to switch between 3 themes. 

We  added additional fixes for office documents and user-registry/SSO in a [🌷🩹 Spring fix release (2026.5.1)](https://github.com/cryptpad/cryptpad/releases/tag/2026.5.1).
